Ashok Gurung

I'm a postdoctoral research associate at Washington State University, working with Prof. Scott Beckman and Prof. Sinisa Mesarovic on multiscale and multiphysics modeling of energy materials.

I received my Ph.D. in Physics from University of Connecticut, where I was advised by Dr. Serge Nakhmanson and completed my M.S. in Physics at Wright State University.

I build multiscale, multiphysics simulations — bridging atomistic, mesoscopic, and continuum methods — to probe transport, mechanics, and microstructure evolution in solid-state batteries, ferroelectrics, and relaxor dielectrics.
